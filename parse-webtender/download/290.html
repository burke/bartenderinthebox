Haiku Martini

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz Sake
  • 1 dash Dry Vermouth
  • 1 Cocktail onion

Mixing instructions:

In a mixing glass half-filled with ice cubes, combine the sake and vermouth. Stir well. Strain into a chilled cocktail glass and garnish with a cocktail onion.


Drink Information:
Category: Cocktail
Alcohol: Alcoholic
Serve in: Cocktail glass
