Cherry Popper

Ingredients:

  • 1 shot Strawberry schnapps
  • Fill with 7-Up
  • Drop in 1 Cherry

Mixing instructions:

Pop cherry into strawberry schnapps / 7-up mix.

Creator/contributor's comments:

This smooth drink is the brainchild of my sexy girlfriend Brenda.
